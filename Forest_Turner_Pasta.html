Ingredients

  • salt
  • water
  • pasta from costco
  • butter
  • cheese

PASTA

Description

Pasta and cheese is a dish with a lot of cheese and pasta.... and more cheese. If you have the really crappy skinny noodles.. throw those away and go back to the store.

Directions

  1. Start boiling water in a pot.
  2. Add a lot of salt to the water.
  3. Once the water is boiling hard, dump in the pasta.
  4. Turn the stove up to a hard boil.
  5. Cook pasta for 12 minutes
  6. Drain pasta and then put the pasta back into the pot. Add cheese and butter. Then add more cheese. DONE!
